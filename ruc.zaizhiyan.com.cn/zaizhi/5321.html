2012同等学力申硕英语模拟试题八

时间:2012-07-11 来源:未知 点击:次
1. Our association, which has consistently pressed for greater employment opportunities for the disabled, will publish ____ proposals in the near future. A. their B. our C. his D. its 2. Had Judy been more careful on the maths exam, she __

　　1. Our association, which has consistently pressed for greater employment opportunities for the disabled, will publish ____ proposals in the near future.

　　A. their B. our C. his D. its

　　2. Had Judy been more careful on the maths exam, she ____ much better results now.

　　A. would be getting　　 B. could have got

　　C. must get D. would get

　　3. Nine is to three _____ three is to one.

　　A. when B. that

　　C. which D. what

　　4. Men differ from animals ____ they can think and speak.

　　A. for which B. for that

　　C. in that 　 　D. in which

　　5. ____ he wanted to go out with his friends at the weekend, he had to stay behind to finish his assignment.

　　A. Much though 　B. Much as

　　C. As much 　D. Though much

　　【参考答案】

　　1【答案】D

　　【解析】Our association publish (its) proposals.主语是Our association，是一个单位，单数名词，可能会理解为这个单位里的人们提出的建议，但不应该这样去理解。

　　2【答案】B

　　【解析】Had Judy been more careful on the exam=If Judy had been more carefully, she (would be getting) better now! 如它解释的一样，这是一个错综复杂句，按照一般理解应该选，could have got,，但这里是表示(NOW)，也就是要强调现在的结果，应该是表示，她现在就可以正在得到更好结果。而如果用could have got,就只是表示一个虚拟的事实，她将得到更好的结果，体现不了NOW的作用。

　　3 【答案】D

　　【解析】Nine is to three (what) three is to one.此处考察what的用法，正如，好像，比如 Water is to fish what air is to human beings。

　　4 【答案】A

　　【解析】in that， 首先它不是定语从句，如果是的话，必须在IN前面加个逗号，构成非限制定语从句，WHICH 表示前面整句话的意思。但如果这样，介词应该也应该改为FOR，或者连接BECAUSE OF。它的解释里说是在……方面，其实"IN THAT"本身就有"因为"的意思。

　　5 【答案】B

　　【解析】Mush as 句型，表示"尽管"的意思，把副词MUCH提前到AS前面，常考的题目了，应该问题不大。
