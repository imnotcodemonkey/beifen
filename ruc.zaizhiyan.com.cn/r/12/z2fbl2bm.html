2011年同等学力考试英语押题

 

 

附：2011年同等学力人员申请硕士学位英语水平全国统一模拟考试答案


　　Part I    Dialogue Communication

　　Section A  Dialogue Completion

　　1-5: C AABB

　　Section B Dialogue Comprehension

　　6-10: BC DB A

　　Part II

　　Section A

　　11-15 BBABA；16-20 BDADA

　　Section B

　　21-25 BCBAD；26-30 CDCCB

　　Part III

　　31-36: DDCBBB  37-42: BBCDDA

　　43-48: ACCCDB  49-54: CCDABD

　　55-60: CCCBBC

　　Part IV Cloze Test

　　61-65: CA DDA．

　　66-70: BADBA

　　71-75: BBDAC

　　Part I Translation

　　Section A

　　人们使用的水资源不到全世界可用水资源的一半。但是，水资源的缺乏和干旱正在造成某些地区的饥荒和贫困，而且工农业的副产品也导致了水源的污染。由于全世界人口在今后50年内可能会翻一翻，因此很多专家认为我们正面临着全球范围的水资源危机。

　　今天我们必须从崭新的经济视角来看待水资源，我们再也不能把水当成一种我们想怎么用就怎么用的资源了。相反，除了穷人的生活用水外，政府应当对水资源的任何其他使用都应标价收费来体现水的价值。这就意味着不仅要对供应成本收费，而且还要对水本身收费。

　　Section B

　　The Chinese have traditionally attached great importance to education, especially the education of children. Many parents have their children enrolled in many various training courses to increase their chances of being admitted to the ideal high schools. However, experts point out that maintaining children's curiosity about and interest in science will promote their intellectual development. A child without any creativity or imagination can hardly go very far.

　　Part II Writing

　　With the stable decline in birth rate and drastic expansion in life span, it is more and more obvious that China is going to become a population aging society, which has aroused the great concern of the general public.

　　The coming of the population aging society will pose great challenges to the development of our society. One of the direct results will be a significant drop in the labor force supply. Labor shortage will affect the productivity of the whole society and undermine the key foundation of the country's economic growth. What's more, young people will face greater pressure of taking care of the elderly since families become smaller and smaller.

　　As far as I'm concerned, population aging has become a serious social problem that deserves the great attention of the government and whole society. I think it cannot be satisfactorily addressed without the joint efforts of the government and whole society. More nursing homes should be set up and sound social security system should be established to take good care of the aged and reduce the burden of the young.
