同等学力申硕必备之虚拟语气

时间:2011-06-01 来源:未知 点击:次 Tag: 同等学力申硕

　　一、 非真实性条件句

　　1. 三种基本形式

　　⑴与现在事实相反

　　如：If I had time, I would go to the movies tonight.

　　⑵与过去事实相反

　　如：If we had arrived in time, we would not have missed the train.

　　⑶与将来事实相反

　　如：If the sun were to rise in the west, I would marry you.

　　2.       含蓄 but for, without = if it were not for

　　= were it not for  现在

　　= if it hadn’t been for  过去

　　= had it not been for

　　如：But for the sun, there could be no life on earth.

　　3．混合型  now, today

　　如：If I had taken my raincoat when I came out this morning, I wouldn’t be wet to the skin now.

　　4．倒装

　　如：If I were young, I would enter the beauty competition.

　　Were I young, I would enter the beauty competition.

　　5．跳层  or, or else, otherwise

　　如：Many wasn’t feeling well, otherwise she wouldn’t have left the meeting so early.

　　二、wish, if only, as if, had/would rather

　　1. wish引导的宾语从句的谓语动词用虚拟语气，用一般过去时表示对现在的虚拟，过去完成时表示对过去，would do表示对将来的一种愿望。

　　如：I wish it wasn’t raining.   现在

　　I wish I could have understood the lecture he gave yesterday.    过去

　　I wish you would explain what you mean.    将来

　　2. if only   要是…多好啊（与wish用法基本相同）

　　⑴ 与现在的事实相反用一般过去时

　　如：If only he were alive.

　　⑵ 与过去的事实相反

　　如：If only I hadn’t lost it!

　　3. as if/though   好象

　　⑴ 表示与现在事实相反的虚拟语气，从句里的动词用过去时

　　如：He acted as if he were an expert.

　　⑵ 表示对过去事实相反的虚拟语气，从句里的动词用过去完成时

　　如：He appeared as if he had known nothing about it.

　　他看上去似乎对此一无所知。

　　★     用于客观陈述，则用陈述语气

　　如：It looks as if it is going to rain.

　　4. would/had rather/sooner  宁愿

　　⑴ 表示对将来的愿望的虚拟语气，从句里的动词用原形

　　如：I would rather that you call on me tomorrow. 我宁愿你明天来看我。

　　⑵ 表示与现在事实相反的虚拟语气，从句里的动词用过去时

　　如：I had rather you posted the letter right away.  我宁愿你马上把信寄了。

　　He would rather that it were summer now.

　　⑶ 表示对过去事实相反的虚拟语气，从句里的动词用过去完成时

　　如：I’d rather he hadn’t told me about it.

　　三、与名词性从句有关的虚拟语气

　　1．主语从句

　　It is/was + 形容词/过去分词 + (should) do

　　这些词有possible, important, advisable, vital, desirable, essential, better, asked, necessary, urgent, imperative, appropriate, commanded, requested, probable, preferable, preferred, insisted, recommended, ordered, required, suggested, resolved

　　如：It is strange that she (should) not come here.

　　2．宾语从句

　　在表示愿望、建议、命令意义的动词之后的宾语从句用虚拟语气should（可省略）+ 动词原形的形式，这些动词有：

　　ask, demand, require, request, urge, suggest, propose, move, vote, decide, recommend, advise, desire, plead, order, object, insist, prefer

　　3． 位语从句

　　suggestion, requirement, proposal, recommendation, desire
